Why Cellulose Nanocrystals?

CNCs combine high performance with sustainability. Their nanoscale structure forms effective barriers against liquids, gases, and particles, while surface charges enable selective filtering. Available in versatile formats, CNCs are easy to integrate and offer a green alternative to synthetic materials.
